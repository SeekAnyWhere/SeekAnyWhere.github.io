SeekAnyWhere LAB

      The Lab

      SeekAnyWhere LAB, affiliated with the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), is dedicated to cutting-edge research in visual localization and 3D vision, with a focus on advancing high-precision, robust environmental perception and spatial intelligence technologies in complex real-world scenarios. The lab focus on multi-sensor fusion, 3D reconstruction, and scene understanding, integrating deep learning with geometry-driven approaches to develop real-time perception and localization systems for dynamic, large-scale environments. These advancements underpin critical applications such as intelligent autonomous systems, augmented/virtual reality (AR/VR), autonomous driving, and digital twins.
